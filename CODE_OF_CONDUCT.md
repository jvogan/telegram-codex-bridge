# Code Of Conduct

This repository is an experimental public project. Be direct, respectful, and technical.

## Expected Behavior

- discuss bugs, risks, and tradeoffs in concrete terms
- keep feedback focused on the code, docs, or behavior being proposed
- assume good intent while still holding a high technical bar
- respect privacy and security boundaries when discussing local secrets, Telegram identities, and staged content

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- posting secrets, private logs, or operator data in public threads
- pushing contributors to reveal tokens, chat IDs, or private infrastructure details
- derailing technical review into interpersonal arguments

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates these standards. Repeated or severe violations may result in removal from collaboration spaces around the project.
